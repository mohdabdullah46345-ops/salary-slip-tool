About Salary Slip Generator

Last updated: 9 June 2026

Salary Slip Generator is a free, easy-to-use online tool that helps small businesses, startups, freelancers, and HR teams create clean, professional salary slips (payslips) in just a few minutes. There is no sign-up, no software to install, and no cost involved.

What We Do

Our goal is to make payroll documentation simple for everyone. Many small employers and individuals struggle to produce a properly formatted payslip. This tool lets you enter your company details, employee information, earnings, and deductions, and instantly generates a well-structured payslip that you can download as a PDF.

Key Features

  • Live preview that updates as you type
  • Company branding with logo upload
  • Complete employee and bank details
  • Customisable earnings and deductions
  • Automatic calculation of gross pay, deductions, and net pay
  • Net amount converted into words (Indian numbering system)
  • One-click PDF download via your browser's print dialog
  • All data stays in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server

Our Commitment to Privacy

We believe your payroll data is private. That is why this tool runs entirely inside your web browser. The information you enter is never sent to us or any third party. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
